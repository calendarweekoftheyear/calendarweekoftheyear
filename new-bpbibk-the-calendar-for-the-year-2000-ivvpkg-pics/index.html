The Calendar for the Year 2000

The year 2000 was a leap year, with 366 days instead of the usual 365. This was because it was a year that was divisible by 400. The leap day was added to the month of February, which had 29 days instead of the usual 28.

The year 2000 was also a special year because it was the first year of the new millennium. The millennium is a period of 1,000 years, and the year 2000 marked the beginning of the third millennium.
